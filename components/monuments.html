MONUMENTS OF ODISHA

Discover the architectural marvels and cultural heritage of ancient Odisha. These magnificent structures showcase the rich history and artistic excellence of the region.

Konark Sun Temple

A 13th-century Sun Temple showcasing remarkable architecture and intricate stone carvings.

Lingaraj Temple

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, representing the peak of Kalinga architecture.

Udayagiri Caves

Historic rock-cut caves featuring beautiful Buddhist and Jain sculptures from ancient times.

Mukteswar Temple

Known as the "Gem of Odisha Architecture" with exquisite stone carvings and sculptures.

Jagannath Temple

One of the four sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus, located in Puri.

Rajarani Temple

Famous for its beautiful sculptures and intricate architectural details from the 11th century.

Dhauli Stupa

A Buddhist monument marking the site of the historic Kalinga War and Emperor Ashoka's transformation.

Brahmeswar Temple

An ancient temple featuring stunning architecture and religious significance in Bhubaneswar.

Ananta Vasudeva Temple

A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna with unique architectural features.

Parasurameswara Temple

One of the oldest temples in Bhubaneswar with remarkable stone carvings.

Khandagiri Caves

Ancient Jain rock-cut caves with historical inscriptions and sculptures.

Vaital Temple

A unique temple with Tantric architectural elements and distinctive design.
