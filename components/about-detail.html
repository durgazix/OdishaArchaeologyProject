ABOUT US

The Department of Archaeology, Government of Odisha, stands as a beacon of archaeological excellence and cultural preservation in eastern India. Established with the noble mission of safeguarding Odisha's irreplaceable archaeological heritage, our department works tirelessly to conserve, research, and promote the state's magnificent ancient monuments and artifacts. Odisha's archaeological legacy spans millennia, from prehistoric settlements to the glorious medieval kingdoms that built iconic structures like the Jagannath Temple in Puri and the Sun Temple at Konark.

Our department operates multiple field offices strategically located across Odisha to conduct systematic excavations, surveys, and conservation work at historically significant sites. We maintain specialized laboratories for artifact analysis, preservation, and restoration using state-of-the-art techniques. Through our comprehensive site museum network, we bring the wonders of Odisha's ancient past directly to communities and tourists, fostering greater awareness and appreciation of our cultural heritage. The department's research initiatives have unveiled remarkable evidence of Odisha's prosperous maritime trade networks, advanced urban settlements, and sophisticated artistic traditions.

We are committed to advancing archaeological knowledge through rigorous fieldwork, detailed documentation, and scholarly publication. Our team of experienced archaeologists, conservators, and support staff collaborates with national and international institutions to ensure that Odisha's archaeological contributions to Indian civilization are properly recognized and preserved. Every excavation, every artifact conserved, and every monument restored represents our dedication to honoring the achievements of our ancestors.

The Department of Archaeology, Government of Odisha, envisions a future where Odisha's ancient heritage continues to inspire, educate, and enrich the lives of present and future generations. We invite scholars, students, and heritage enthusiasts to explore the remarkable story of Odisha's archaeological treasures and to join us in our mission to preserve this invaluable legacy for humanity.
