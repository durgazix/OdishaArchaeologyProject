EXCLUSIVE LIBRARY FOR SCHOLARS

Odisha State Archaeology Department has one of the finest archaeological libraries in the country, housing over 13,300 books on Archaeology, Anthropology, Art, History, Epigraphy and Palaeography.

Reprints of rare journals like Indian Antiquary and Asiatic Researches are preserved here for advanced research.

The library is regularly updated with journals, digital archives, and reference materials for scholars and researchers.
