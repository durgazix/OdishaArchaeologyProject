BOOK & JOURNALS

The Department Press is exclusively for bringing out books in Odia and English on Epigraphy, Monuments, Excavation reports, District Archaeological Guide books, Museum Guide books and Temple Monographs.

The department has so far published 285 books. A quarterly journal "Kalvettu" is also being published.

Nearly 250 publications are available in the official website www.odishaarchaeology.gov.in of the department.
